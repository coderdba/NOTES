ELASTIC
==========
ON MAC

Download tar.gz file and unzip to, say, ~/opt/elasticearch/<unzip here>

Start (foreground): opt/elasticsearch/elasticsearch-7.9.1/bin/elasticsearch
Start (daemon): opt/elasticsearch/elasticsearch-7.9.1/bin/elasticsearch -d
Logs: opt/elasticsearch/elasticsearch-7.9.1/logs
Access: http://localhost:9200/
